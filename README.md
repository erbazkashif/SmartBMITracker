# Smart BMI Tracker 📊

**Smart BMI Tracker** is a professional, high-performance health monitoring application developed by **Erbaz Labs**. Designed with a focus on user experience and medical accuracy, this app provides deep insights into your physical health through real-time data analysis.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Advanced Metrics Suite**: 
  - **BMI Calculation**: Instant results for both Metric (cm/kg) and Imperial (ft/lb) units.
  - **Body Fat Percentage**: Advanced calculation using the US Navy method based on age and gender.
  - **BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)**: Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to calculate your body's energy needs at rest.
  - **Ideal Weight Range**: Automatically suggests your perfect weight for a healthy BMI.
  - **Daily Calorie Needs**: Estimating your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) to help with weight management.
- **Interactive UI/UX**:
  - **Real-time Sync**: Age slider and direct input fields stay perfectly synchronized.
  - **Visual Gauges**: High-quality custom gauges for weight, height, and BMI feedback.
  - **Weight Trends**: Integrated line charts to track your fitness journey over time.
- **Smart Data Persistence**: Remembers your last profile (Gender, Age, Height, Weight) so you don't have to re-enter it.
- **Modern Branding**: Includes a custom-designed professional adaptive icon and a clean, premium blue-themed interface.

## 📐 Formulas Used

This app uses globally recognized fitness formulas:
- **BMI**: `weight (kg) / [height (m)]²`
- **BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor)**: 
  - *Male*: `(10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5`
  - *Female*: `(10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161`
- **Body Fat %**: `(1.20 × BMI) + (0.23 × age) - (10.8 × gender) - 5.4`
